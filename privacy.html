Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2025  ·  CleanCopy by Arksoft

Overview

CleanCopy is built on a simple principle: your data stays on your device. Neither the web tool nor the Mac app transmits, stores, logs, or shares any information about you or the URLs you clean.

Web Tool

The CleanCopy web tool at cleancopy.arksoft.xyz runs entirely in your browser. URLs you paste into the tool are processed locally by JavaScript and are never sent to any server. We do not use cookies, session storage, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking on this page. No account is required and no data is retained after you close the tab.

Mac App

CleanCopy for Mac reads your system clipboard and writes a cleaned URL back to it — entirely on-device. The app does not make any network requests, does not transmit clipboard contents anywhere, and does not collect crash reports, diagnostics, or usage analytics. The only data persisted between launches is your enabled/disabled preference, stored locally in macOS UserDefaults on your own machine.

App Store Purchase

Purchases of the Mac app are handled entirely by Apple through the Mac App Store. We receive no payment information, no email address, and no personal details from Apple beyond the aggregate, anonymized download statistics that Apple provides to all developers.

Children's Privacy

CleanCopy does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As no data is collected from any user, this policy applies equally to all age groups.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of CleanCopy after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
